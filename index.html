Research

My research revolves around fundamental optimization problems motivated by networks and data centers, e.g. routing, caching, scheduling and graph partitioning. Online variants of these problems model dynamic and time-related nature of networks particularly well. Practical applications include self-adjusting networks, virtual machine placement and migration, cache memory management, and optical networks.
